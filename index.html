Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you service?

We currently service Auckland and surrounding areas including North Shore, Manukau, Waitakere, and Rodney districts. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

Are your cleaning products really eco-friendly?

Yes! We use certified environmentally responsible cleaning products that are biodegradable, non-toxic, and safe for both people and pets. Our products meet strict environmental standards without compromising on cleaning power.

Can you clean after business hours?

Absolutely. We specialize in after-hours cleaning to ensure zero disruption to your business operations. Our teams work evenings, nights, and weekends based on your schedule preferences.

How do you ensure quality control?

We conduct regular supervisor inspections, use detailed checklists, and provide client feedback systems. All our staff undergo thorough training and background checks. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee.

How do I get a quote?

Simply fill out our online form, call us, or request a free site visit. We'll assess your space, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote within 24 hours.
